Welcome to the new **Podunk Libertarians** website!

We're the local affiliate of the Libertarian Party, serving Jerkwater and Unincorporated Podunk. Whether you've been a libertarian for decades or you're just discovering the ideas of individual liberty and limited government, you're welcome here.

## What we do

- **Monthly meetups** at Jerkwater Brewing Co. — first Thursday of every month at 6:00 PM. Come for the conversation, stay for the beer.
- **News and advocacy** — we track local issues that affect your freedom and let you know how to make your voice heard.
- **Community** — we're your neighbors who believe in live and let live.

## Get involved

The best way to start is to [join our mailing list]({{ site.mailing_list_url }}) or come to a [meetup]({{ site.eventbrite_url }}). No commitment required — just show up.

If you'd like to support our work, you can [donate here]({{ site.anedot_url }}).

See you at the next meetup!

*— The Podunk Libertarians*
