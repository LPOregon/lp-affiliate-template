The Podunk Libertarians are proud to support the following candidates in the 2024 election cycle. We believe in expanding liberty at every level of government — from city hall to the statehouse.

## Jane Liberty — State House District 42

Jane is a small business owner and lifelong Podunk County resident running on a platform of lower taxes, reduced regulation, and criminal justice reform. She has been a Libertarian since 2018 and currently serves as Chair of the Podunk Libertarians.

**Campaign site:** [janeliberty2024.com](#)

## Bob Freedom — County Commissioner, Seat 3

Bob is a retired engineer and fiscal conservative who believes local government should do less and cost less. He is challenging an incumbent who has supported three consecutive property tax increases.

**Campaign site:** [bobfreedom2024.com](#)
