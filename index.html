The Party of Principle

Self-Ownership

Every person has the right to control their own body, speech, actions, and property. No one — including government — may initiate force or fraud against another.

Free Markets

Voluntary exchange and cooperation produce more prosperity and justice than any centrally planned system. Economic freedom is inseparable from personal freedom.

Limited Government

Government exists only to protect individual rights. When it goes beyond that role, it becomes the greatest threat to those rights.
